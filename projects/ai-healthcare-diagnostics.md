# AI-Driven Healthcare Diagnostics Project

## Overview
An instructional and research-focused project about AI-assisted healthcare diagnosis, cancer detection, diagnostic efficiency, and medical education.

## Problem
Healthcare systems often face delayed diagnosis, limited access to specialists, and inefficiencies in decision-making. AI diagnostic tools can help support clinicians by identifying patterns, improving triage, and reducing delays.

## My Role
I researched AI-driven diagnostic tools and helped shape an educational deliverable that explains the value, limitations, and use cases of AI in healthcare.
