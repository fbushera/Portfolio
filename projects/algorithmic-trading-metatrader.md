# Algorithmic Trading / MetaTrader Automation

## Overview
A collection of algorithmic trading and FinTech automation projects using MQL5, Python bridges, and rule-based Expert Advisors for MetaTrader strategy development and testing.

## Problem
Manual trading strategies can be inconsistent, difficult to test, and hard to execute with discipline. Algorithmic trading tools allow strategy rules, risk controls, and trade management logic to be converted into programmable systems.

## My Role
I worked on Expert Advisor logic, indicator-based strategy design, risk-management parameters, backtesting workflows, and Python-to-MetaTrader automation concepts.

## Technologies
- MQL5
- Python
- MetaTrader 5
- Expert Advisors
- Algorithmic trading logic
- Strategy testing and automation

## Key Work Completed
- Developed rule-based Expert Advisors and indicators
- Worked with XAUUSD-focused scalping and day-trading strategies
- Built configurable trade parameters such as lot size, risk controls, stop loss, and take profit
- Explored Python bridge concepts for connecting external logic to trading workflows
- Used backtesting to evaluate and iterate on strategy behavior
