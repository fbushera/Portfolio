# FoodieTok / Review-Based Social App

## Overview
FoodieTok is a review-based social platform concept where users can create profiles, follow others, and post photo or video reviews of restaurants, activities, and businesses. Businesses can also have professional-style accounts to promote services and connect with users.

## Problem
People often rely on scattered reviews, social media posts, and word-of-mouth recommendations when deciding where to eat or what activities to try. FoodieTok brings social discovery and reviews into one mobile-first experience.

## My Role
I worked on the mobile MVP, Firebase integration, authentication flow, profile management, real-time feed behavior, and media upload features.
